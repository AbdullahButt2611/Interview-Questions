# Measure 4L Water Using 3L and 5L Jars
`Softpers Interactive` • `Plintron Global Technology Solutions` • `Zeus Learning`

1. Fill the **5L jar**.
2. Pour water from the **5L jar** into the **3L jar** until the 3L jar is full.  
   - 5L jar now has **2L** left.
3. Empty the **3L jar**.
4. Pour the **2L** from the 5L jar into the **3L jar**.
5. Fill the **5L jar** again.
6. Pour water from the **5L jar** into the **3L jar** until it becomes full.  
   - The 3L jar already has **2L**, so it takes **1L** more.

**Result:** The **5L jar now contains exactly 4L of water.**

> This problem is also called as **Water Jug Problem**
